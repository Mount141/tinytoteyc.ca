About Us

Tiny Tot Early Years Centres provide child care and early learning for children from infancy through pre-kindergarten. With four locations in Charlottetown and Summerside, PE, we focus on communication with families, community activities, and a nurturing environment for growth and exploration.

Age-Based Programs

  • Infants
  • Toddlers
  • Preschool
  • Pre-Kindergarten

Programs are designed to support learning, development, social skills, and independence. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

Our Services

We offer a range of early years services, including:

  • Play-based learning activities
  • Healthy meals aligned with Canada’s Food Guide
  • Community field trips & outdoor play
  • Parent-educator communication and events

Educators are certified in Early Childhood Education and regularly trained in safety and development

What Families Say

Parents appreciate the nurturing environment, individualized attention, and positive experiences their children have at Tiny Tot Centres.
