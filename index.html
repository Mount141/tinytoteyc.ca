Welcome To Tiny Tot Early Years Centres

Promote your child's self-esteem and education with the help of Tiny Tot Early Years Centres. Our Early Years programs are inclusive for all children from infants to pre-kindergarten.

With an emphasis on parent/educator communication, we encourage your input and welcome you to take part in activities and meetings, and consider being a member of our Parent Advisory Community.

Community Activities & Nutrition

Tiny Tot Early Years Centres work with other community professionals to allow children to explore activities outside the centres. We schedule field trips and excursions to help children learn both inside and outside the classroom environment.

Proper nutrition for your child is important to us. We provide healthy, balanced meals and snacks that fuel growing minds and bodies.

About Us

Meet Our Charlottetown & Summerside Certified Childhood Educators

Tiny Tot Early Years Centres offer child care at four separate locations. The Tiny Tot Kensington Road centre has been licensed since 1972, Rainbow has been licensed since 2003, Tiny Tot Pope Ave has been licensed since 2006 and Playtime has been licensed since 1980.

We are a family oriented business with a strong emphasis on parent and educator communications.

We provide our child care in bright, roomy buildings with large outdoor facilities for playtime.

Transportation for field trips and community activities are provided by our centres. With fully certified educators on staff, all programs are designed specifically for age-appropriate development.

Our Programs

Infants: Gentle care, stimulation, and nurturing in a safe environment.

Toddlers: Exploration and development through play-based learning and socialization.

Preschool / Pre-K: Early literacy, numeracy, and emotional development.

What We Offer

üçº Infant Program

Our infant program provides children opportunities to experience a variety of music, art, literacy, sensory play, and physical activities both indoors and outdoors. Through these experiences as well as a safe and secure environment, positive relationships form between children and educators.

Families receive documentation (photos, videos, art displays, portfolios) and a journal highlighting all activities and special details (naptime, toileting, baby‚Äôs firsts, etc.).

üë∂ Toddler Program

We foster toddlers‚Äô curiosity through music, dance, art, literacy, sensory play, and physical activities. Educators support children in developing toilet independence and self-help skills.

Families receive documentation of activities and a journal highlighting special details of the day (toilet training, naptime, eating habits, etc.).

üé® Preschool Program

Hands-on learning engages three-year-olds in sensory play, outdoor learning, and fine motor skill development. Children explore dance, yoga, running, climbing, imaginative play, and social interactions.

Families receive documentation of activities indoors, outdoors, and on field trips.

üìö Pre-Kindergarten Program

Pre-K supports independence, problem-solving, emotional development, and social skills. Children participate in community integration through field trips, events, and guest speakers.

Families receive documentation of all activities, both indoors, outdoors, and on field trips.
